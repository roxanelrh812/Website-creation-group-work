About mock up interview:

Golden Future provide you with a mock up interview, which helps you to better understand the pinpoint of the interview process and the style preference of different top rated business schools.

Price: 40 euros per hour

Before the mock up interview, Golden Future will provide you with the interview guide classified by the business schools that you want to apply.
Interview guide covers huge amounts of information include:

  • 1. The background information of the school.
  • 2.The construction of a business school interview.
  • 3.What kind of questions the interviewer are prone to ask during the interview?
  • 4.How to tell a good story based on your experiences?
  • 5.How to better show the interviewer your strengths?
  • 6.Preference of various business schools.
Etc.

Interview tutors: An average of 5 years of experience in tutoring the students.

The interview tutors will design the application pack for the students and provide them with several one-on-one mock interviews. Also, a specific interview guide will be given to the student based on his or her target schools.
