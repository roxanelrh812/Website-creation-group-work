One step closer to your dream

Since 2015, Golden Future has helped over 800+ young and ambitious students to enter their dream school and receive top companies’ offers they have been longing for. Our dedicated mentors carefully analyze each profile individually and help them find their uniqueness and shining traits.

Xinyi

Chinese student Xinyi graduated from a 2-tier university in China and but always dreamt for the best. She turned to Golden Future for a personal profile analysis and CV review. GF helped her develop a unique personal story-telling and receive admission offers from ESSEC, ESCP, and Bocconi.

Taiwanese student of Essec Business School, though not fluently speaking French, Yu-kai always wanted to join a top French company. GF tailor-made a french interview bootcamp for him and helped him leave an amazing impression during the interview. In the end, Yu-kai successfully received an internship offer from L’Oréal.
